Market Tiers

Prior to Broadcast September 2016, Comscore reporting of demographic segments varied by market tier. Effective with Broadcast September 2016, market Tiers A, B, C, and D were replaced by Tier E to standardize demographic availability across all markets, except the five markets listed below, where demographic segments remain unavailable.
